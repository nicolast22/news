Why is the Lineage the Best E-Jet?


Even though I always preferred airliners to business jets, I have to admit that I’ve completely fallen in love with the Lineage 1000—it’s now my go-to airliner! It’s the ultimate E-Jet that does it all. Here are a few highlights:

  1. Cross oceans with more powerful CF34-10E7-B engines, a custom fuel system, and two auxiliary tanks, giving it an 8,500 km range! Here's a handy guide to long-range flights.

  2. Highly intricate Airstairs for easy passenger access at smaller airports without jetways.

  3. Perfectly crafted luxury interior based on a real Lineage, featuring 50+ bespoke materials, ultra-high-resolution textures, five unique zones, and even a bedroom—unlike anything seen in X-Plane before.

  4. Four distinct liveries, each with its own custom, realistic interior.

  5. Fully customizable cabin lighting, including 28 individually adjustable spotlights, upwash/downwash ambient lighting, and custom ceiling/floor lights—all controlled via a pop-out touchscreen.

  6. Five working cabin TVs, featuring a pre-departure slideshow and flight info.

  7. Interactive cabin: animated blinds, tables, doors, and more—all with custom sounds.

  8. Auto-stow features: All animations and lights automatically adjust for taxi, takeoff, landing, and turbulence.

  9. Optional SATCOM antenna for inflight connectivity & broadcasting.

  10. Configurable window layout—choose plugged or open windows.

  11. Highly customizable—personalize the livery, cabin interior, TV screens, and even the in-flight magazine.

  12. Plus everything the E-Jets can do, including full autopilot automation, custom FMS, and the Steep Approach system for airports like London City.

So no—it’s not just an E190 with a fancy cabin. This is a true luxury airliner, and it took nearly two years to perfect! I’ve poured all my skills, love (and a little sweat) into this, alongside our incredibly talented team, to create the best add-on we’ve ever made.
